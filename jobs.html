    SENIOUR SOFTWARE ENGINEER

    DATA SCIENTIST

    As a data scientist, you'll extract, analyse and interpret large amounts of data from a range of sources, using algorithmic, data mining, artificial intelligence, machine learning and statistical tools, to make it accessible to businesses. Once you've interpreted the data, you'll present your results using clear and engaging language. You'll use your technical, analytical and communication skills to collect and examine data to help a business find patterns and solve problems. This can be for many purposes, for example, predicting what customers will buy or tackling plastic pollution.
